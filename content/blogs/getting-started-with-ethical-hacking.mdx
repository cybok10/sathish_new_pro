# Getting Started with Ethical Hacking: A Beginner's Guide

## Introduction

Ethical hacking, also known as penetration testing, is the practice of testing
a computer system, network, or web application to identify vulnerabilities
that could be exploited by attackers.

## The Golden Rule  

> **"With great power comes great responsibility."**

Never scan, probe, or attack any system without **explicit, written permission**.  
Unauthorized hacking is illegal and unethical.
